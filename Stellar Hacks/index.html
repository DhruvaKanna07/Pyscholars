Who can come? How do I form a team?

Stellar Hacks is open to all high schoolers, even ones outside the Bay Area. Although you are welcome to form your own teams before coming to the hackathon, you don't need a team to join.

What if I don't know how to code?

Don't worry if you don't have any prior coding experience! We will have workshops and short lessons to teach participants the fundamentals of coding. Even without prior coding experience you may be able to play another pivotal role on the team.
